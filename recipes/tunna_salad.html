Tunna Salad

Description

This quick, satisfying tuna salad comes together in minutes using just three simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • ¼ cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Mash tuna, creamy salad dressing, and relish in a small bowl with a fork until combined. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  3. Serve it whit a piece of bread
